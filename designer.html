Sam

Sam Concept is a Ukrainian bridal and evening fashion label. In Ukrainian “WONÁ” means “she”. This name emphasizes the brand’s commitment to its clients – women – who are also the main inspiration for the dresses. WONÁ believes that women are true muses and aims to create dresses that embody their most intimate and extravagant desires. Finding the perfect gown allows women to reveal their own unique beauty. This is why WONÁ strives to satisfy and exceed the expectations of brides and fashionistas all over the world. As a result, WONÁ is continuously capturing the hearts of brides with their extraordinary designs, helping women highlight their unique style and personality on their special day.

Havana

Havana is inspired by the harmony and balance of Neoclassical art and architecture and uses modern silhouettes to accentuate the figure and contour the shape of the body. The Brand Name is derived from the Ancient Greek word “Elysium”, meaning a place or state of perfect happiness. Timeless and gorgeous, our lines are sure to amaze.
